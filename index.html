Kinder

Visit Kinder Live | Watch Video Demo

Ruby on Rails 5 | JS/JQuery | Materialize | TRAVIS CI

APIs & Integrations: OAuth, Google API, Zipcode API, S3

Kinder was created to address two problems plaguing moms: an alternative solution to 50 reply-all email threads to manage their playdate & finding other moms they connect with beyond the commonality of having children.

We designed Kinder with mobile in mind and had 88% test coverage. Kinder was developed RESTfully and using Agile workflow.

Features include:

  • Google sign-in and calendar integration
  • Live messaging
  • "Kinder" - swipe through moms in the area and find connections
  • Playdate scheduling & friending system
  • Google Embeded Maps on Events
  • Ability to upload profile photos
  • Parent Profile with user tested questions

FievStarMovies

Visit FievstarMovies Live | Watch Video Demo

Ruby on Rails 5 | JS/JQuery | Materialize | TRAVIS CI

APIs & Integrations: Movie Database API

This was a simulated client project in which the client requested a review site that was based on Fievel from An American Tail. Built on combining the best parts of IMDB, Rotten Tomatoes, and Yelp; the client wanted to create a site that helped avid movie fans decide what to watch next.

We followed RESTful practices, achieved 94% test coverage, and used Agile workflow.

Features include:

  • Search & Filterable Movies
  • Review System
  • Trusted Reviewer Algorithm
  • Commentable Reviews
  • Playdate scheduling & friending system
